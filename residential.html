Roof Replacement

When repairs won't cut it anymore, a full roof replacement is the right call. We handle everything from tear-off to final inspection — so you get a roof that's built to last 30, 40, even 50 years.

Our replacement process starts with a thorough inspection of your decking, rafters, and existing system. We fix any structural issues before installing your new roof — no shortcuts, no hidden problems left behind.

What's Included:

  • Full tear-off and debris removal
  • Decking inspection and repair
  • New synthetic or felt underlayment
  • All new flashing and drip edge
  • Ice and water shield in critical areas
  • Your choice of roofing system
  • Final cleanup and nail sweep

Cost-Effective Option

Re-Roofing (Roof Overlay)

A roof overlay installs new shingles directly over your existing layer — saving time and labor costs when your deck is still in solid shape.

Not every roof qualifies. We inspect your current roof first to make sure an overlay is the right call for your home. If it's not, we'll be honest with you and explain why a full replacement is better.

Re-Roofing Is Right When:

  • Only one existing layer of shingles
  • Roof decking is structurally sound
  • No widespread moisture damage
  • You want to save on disposal costs
  • Minimal structural repairs needed

Repairs

Roof Leak Repairs

A small leak ignored is a big problem waiting to happen. Water intrusion causes rot, mold, and structural damage fast — especially in the heat of Southern Utah summers.

We diagnose leaks accurately and fix the actual cause — not just the symptom. No band-aid repairs that leave you calling again in six months.

Common Repairs We Handle:

  • Flashing failures at chimneys and vents
  • Damaged or missing shingles or tiles
  • Valley and ridge repairs
  • Skylight and pipe boot leaks
  • Storm and wind damage repairs
  • Flat roof membrane repairs
