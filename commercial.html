Most Popular Commercial System

TPO Roofing Systems

Thermoplastic Polyolefin (TPO) is the most widely installed commercial flat roofing membrane in the industry — and for good reason. It's durable, energy-efficient, and heat-weldable for completely watertight seams.

TPO reflects UV rays, reducing cooling costs in Southern Utah's intense heat. It's available in multiple thicknesses and attachment methods for virtually any commercial building type.

Our TPO Services Include:

  • New TPO installation — fully adhered or mechanically fastened
  • TPO re-roofing over existing systems
  • 60-mil and 80-mil membrane options
  • TPO repairs and seam welding
  • Insulation board and recovery board installation
  • Penetration and detail work

Premium Membrane System

PVC Membrane Roofing

PVC (polyvinyl chloride) membranes are highly chemical-resistant, making them an excellent choice for restaurants, food processing, or buildings with rooftop HVAC units and grease exposure.

PVC is heat-welded like TPO but offers superior chemical resistance and long-term flexibility, making it a premium option for demanding commercial applications.

PVC Applications:

  • Restaurants and food service buildings
  • Buildings with chemical or grease exposure
  • New commercial construction
  • High-performance re-roofing projects
  • Fully adhered and mechanically fastened systems

Premium Performance

FiberTite Membrane Roofing

FiberTite is a high-performance membrane roofing system known for its exceptional puncture resistance, chemical resistance, and proven longevity. It's one of the most durable single-ply membranes available.

FiberTite systems come with strong manufacturer warranties and are engineered for demanding commercial and industrial environments where standard membranes fall short.

FiberTite Advantages:

  • Superior puncture and tear resistance
  • Excellent chemical and UV resistance
  • Strong manufacturer warranty programs
  • Ideal for industrial and high-demand applications
  • Heat-welded seams for maximum watertightness

Extend Your Roof's Life

Roof Coatings & Restoration

If your commercial roof has life left in it but is showing signs of wear, a roof coating system can restore its waterproofing performance and extend its life by 10–20 years at a fraction of replacement cost.

We assess your existing roof to determine if a coating restoration is the right call — and if it is, we prepare the surface properly and install a high-performance coating system that protects your investment.

Coating Benefits:

  • Significant cost savings vs. full replacement
  • Extends roof life 10–20 years
  • Improves energy efficiency
  • Reduces interior cooling costs
  • Can qualify for tax incentives
